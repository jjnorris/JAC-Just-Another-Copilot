# Compliance triage

## goal
Surface obligations and conflict points early without pretending to offer legal sign-off.

## when to use
- Regulated domains, auth, secrets, data handling, licensing, and distributed software decisions.

## prerequisites
- Task scope.
- Known domain or distribution context.

## steps
1. Detect sensitive domain markers.
2. Classify the issue as warning or blocker.
3. Note obligations, review needs, and safer alternatives.
4. Add rollback or disable notes for risky changes.
5. Escalate incompatible conflicts.

## pause points
- When a blocker appears.
- When distribution goals and license terms conflict.

## evidence to collect
- domain markers
- obligation notes
- escalation note if needed

## stop conditions
- Explicit incompatible compliance or license conflict.
- Unsafe secret handling path.

## escalation or review path
- Route blockers to the appropriate owner or counsel.

## final report contract
State the domain triggers, warnings vs blockers, and any safer substitute proposed.
